FAQ

Common questions

Is Wise actually free to use?

Yes, creating a Wise account is completely free. You only pay fees when you send money or convert currencies. There are no monthly subscription fees or hidden charges.

What's the catch with the 15 GBP bonus?

When you sign up using our referral link and complete a qualifying transfer (usually 200 GBP or equivalent), you'll receive 15 GBP in fee credit. This can be used on future transfers. The referral also receives credit.

How does Wise make money if fees are so low?

Wise charges a small, transparent percentage fee (typically 0.35-1.5%) on each transfer. This is much lower than banks because Wise doesn't hide markup in the exchange rate — they use the real mid-market rate.

Is my money safe with Wise?

Yes. Wise is authorized by financial regulators in multiple countries and holds your funds in segregated accounts. They're regulated by the FCA (UK), FinCEN (US), and other authorities worldwide.

How long do transfers take?

Most transfers are instant or arrive within hours. The exact time depends on the currency route and payment method. Bank transfers typically take 1-2 days, while card payments are usually instant.

Can I use Wise in my country?

Wise is available in most countries worldwide. You can check availability for your specific country on their website. Most major currencies are supported across 160+ countries.
